Rules of Baseball

  1. Length of Game
    • 9 Innings of Play
    • Each inning allows each team to bat, home team batting last
  2. Winning a Game
    • Team with the most runs scored wins
    • Runs are scored when a runner crosses home plate
  3. Pitching
    • Pitcher can throw many types of pitches
    • 4-Seam Fastball
    • 2-Seam Fastball
    • Curveball
    • Slider
    • Changeup
